The 4 pillars (or legs) of dharma according to Hindu philosophy are:

Satya (Truth/Truthfulness) - Being truthful in thought, speech and action
Tapas (Austerity/Self-discipline) - Practicing self-control and discipline in body, mind and spirit
Shaucha (Purity/Cleanliness) - Maintaining purity of body, mind, thoughts and surroundings
Daya (Compassion/Mercy) - Showing kindness, empathy and mercy towards all living beings
